Architectural visualization

Here are gathered a series of interior and exterior renderings. Some of them were used to present personal ideas while the others are architectural studies aimed at developing my technique.

Each study is carried out with the same approach as a photographer. Composition, lighting and texturing are essential in order to make sensible the visuals produced. In addition to the work done in modeling softwares and rendering engines, each image goes through post-production to accentuate its qualities.

The different steps of the process are also motivated by the search for references that feed the final plastic aspects of the renderings made.
