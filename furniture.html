Furniture visualization

Here are gathered a series of renderings for a furniture design project done in collaboration with a fellow metallurgist.

Due to limitations imposed by the tools used for prodcution, each piece follows a generative process based on the cutting, folding and bending of steel.

Prototypes were produces for the different designs. Final polished pieces have yet to be made.
